Events

What We do

We hold a multitude of activities in a variety of fields to engage students from all the departments. We strongly strive to expose students to competitive environments, therefore assisting them in developing their technical and non-technical skills.

SRiSHTi

SRiSHTi, hosted by the IEEE SC 12951, is a technical symposium comprising several events, workshops, hackathons among many more.

Atom

Atom is an annual event where the five circuitry branches of PSG College of Technology coalesce and host a variety of different events.

Inauguration

The Inaugural event marks the start of the next operational year for the IEEE Students Chapter 12951.

Valedictory

The Valedictory event marks the end of a productive and fulfilling year for the IEEE Students Chapter 12951.
